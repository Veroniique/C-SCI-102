At Seattle Phone Tech we believe in creating sustainable products with recycled resources. We are still in the process of making all our products fully recycled, but we try our best to incorporate recycled materials in what we make. The recycled products we produce are intended as add-ons for your phone.


We create phone covers for protection, phone sleeves for those who run, phone wallets to store your money on a night out, wireless charging stations and awesome PopSockets to have full grip on your phone while taking awesome selfies with your friends or family. Currently we are working hard on creating an airpod container that we are planning to release in July 2023. Subscribe to our mailing list for more details on our products and future promotions!
