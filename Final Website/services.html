SERVICES WE OFFER


Customer Service

Our customer's happiness are our number one priority. We want to make sure that you get the best service from us. Our staff are very friendly, helpful, reliable and great listeners to help fit your needs. You can make use of our customer service both from in-person and online. For in person customer service, visit our stores between 9AM - 5PM. If you prefer to have a virtual customer service experience you can call us at (206) 878-3710 or you can schedule a virtual meet online through our website. If you are not happy with the product you purchased, you are more than welcome to return it with proof of purchase from the last 30 days.



Service Plans

We have plans for everyone! At Seattle Phone Tech we specialize in indivudal, education, home and workspace plans to fit all our customer needs. Let us know the type of service you are looking for and we will make sure to guide you in the right direction or create a customizable plan that works for you.
